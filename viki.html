Meet VIKI

Think it.  Say it.  Build it.

A local, voice-first AI brainstorming partner built on Claude. She listens continuously, thinks out loud with you, and captures every exchange so no idea gets lost between the moment it's spoken and the moment it gets built.

VIKI bridges the gap between raw creative thinking and real implementation. Brainstorm by voice, then push sessions directly into Claude Code as structured markdown references, turning conversations into buildable context.

Continuous Listening

VIKI stays on, always ready. No wake words, no interruptions. Just speak and she captures it all.

Thinks Out Loud With You

Zero Ideas Lost

Capabilities

Everything VIKI can do

Claude Code Integration

Push any session directly into Claude Code as a structured markdown reference. Your spoken brainstorm becomes buildable context with one command.

Supabase Session Sync

Every session automatically syncs to your Supabase instance. Your ideas are accessible across every device, every context, every build environment.

Runs Entirely Local

VIKI runs on your machine. No cloud processing of your raw voice data, no latency surprises. Your thinking stays yours.

Natural AI Voice

VIKI speaks back via ElevenLabs, delivering studio-quality, natural voice responses. Brainstorming feels like a real conversation, not a command prompt.
