What is Cascade

Long-form In.
Short-form Out.

AI-Powered Social Video Automation

Cascade ingests your long-form video content and runs it through a coordinated pipeline of AI agents. Each agent owns a specific stage: transcription, copywriting, brand voice, clip selection, thumbnail creation, scheduling, publishing, and performance feedback.

The result: platform-native clips, captions, and thumbnails delivered to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and LinkedIn, automatically, on your schedule, in your brand voice.

Under the Hood

End-to-End System Architecture

Seven coordinated layers move every piece of content from raw video to published post. Each layer is owned by a dedicated agent. Nothing overlaps. Nothing gets missed.

Layer 01

Ingestion

Raw video, audio extraction, Whisper transcription, and metadata intake in parallel.

Layer 02

AI Core

Claude orchestrates clip selection, voice-aligned copywriting, and all downstream directives.

Layer 03

Production

FFmpeg clips, caption burn-in, thumbnail generation, and format adaptation per platform.

Layer 04

Packaging

SEO tagging, copy formatting, content bundling, and optional human review before publish.

Layer 05

Scheduling

AI-optimized publish windows, queue management, and notification bus for every channel.

Layer 06

Publishing

Direct API publish to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn simultaneously.

Layer 07

Feedback Loop

Performance data flows back to the AI core. Every run learns from the last.

Built for Production

Built to Scale With You

From first clip to thousands of posts a month, Cascade's architecture handles it.

Brand Voice Memory

Qdrant vector database stores your tone, vocabulary, and style permanently. Switch between multiple brands without missing a beat.

AI Disclosure Built In

2026 platform requirements met at publish time. Automatic disclosure flags and C2PA content provenance metadata for every post.

Reinforcement Learning

Nova feeds real performance data back into the pipeline. Cascade learns from every post and improves clip selection, timing, and copy automatically.

Multimodal Understanding

GPT-4o Vision and Gemini 2.0 Flash analyze keyframes directly: scene descriptions, sentiment, and highlight scores in a single pass.

Virality Scoring

Every clip gets a virality prediction score trained on millions of short-form videos. Iris ranks and selects only your highest-potential moments.

Modular Microservices

Each agent runs as an independent, event-driven service. Scale any stage independently. Swap tech without touching the whole stack.
